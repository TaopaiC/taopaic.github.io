[Fixed] Ubuntu Feisty & Libxcb 的問題

| Comments

之前提到 libxcb 的問題, ubuntu feisty 終於有新的更新, 不用自己動手了.

libxcb 1.0-1.1ubuntu1 暫時避免這個問題. 預設是關掉檢查lock的機制, 如果要啟用的話, 只要設定LIBXCB_NO_SLOPPY_LOCK為任何值即可.

libxcb 1.0-1.1ubuntu2 則是修正前述patch在building時沒被使用的問題 (Bug #90757).

附上libxcb的ChangeLog

libxcb (1.0-1.1ubuntu2) feisty; urgency=low

  • debian/patches/: Create & place 100_allow_sloppy_lock.diff in quilt’s series index. Closes LP: #90757

– Daniel T Chen Thu, 8 Mar 2007 20:47:14 -0500

libxcb (1.0-1.1ubuntu1) feisty; urgency=low

[ Timo Aaltonen ]
* debian/patches:
– 100_allow_sloppy_lock.diff from Novell, workaround to the various ‘Assertion `c->xlib.lock’ failed”‘ -bugs by setting an environment variable LIBXCB_ALLOW_SLOPPY_LOCK to any value and the check will simply be ignored.
* debian/{control,rules}:
– add quilt to Build-deps, and patchsys-quilt.mk to rules.
– change Maintainer address.

[ Martin Pitt ]
* 100_allow_sloppy_lock.diff: Reverse the patch logic for Feisty: Use sloppy locking by default for now, because we won’t have time to discover and fix all broken apps. Setting LIBXCB_NO_SLOPPY_LOCK will enable the strict behaviour. This should be dropped right at the opening of Feisty+1.

– Timo Aaltonen Wed, 7 Mar 2007 08:27:01 +0100
